Organic Water Filter

For a competition, a group of friends and I wanted to help communities on the outskirts of our city that relied on unsanitary water from underground sources. After extensive research and experimentation, including sending samples to a professional lab for analysis, we ended up finding a solution that was effective in both filtration and cost. The filter was built with layers of coconut husk and seashells and other materials easily available in the region. The filtered water, while still not drinkable, was much safer to use in daily cleaning and cooking scenarios.
